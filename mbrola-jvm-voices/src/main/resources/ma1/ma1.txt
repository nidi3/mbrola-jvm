1. Brief description of MA1 database
-------------------------------------------------------
MA1 is a Malay female diphone database provided in the
context of the MBROLA project (http://tcts.fpms.ac.be/synthesis).

It consists of 801 diphones.

MA1 database was recorded, segmented and processed by 
Kow Weng Onn (kwonn@mimos.my) at the Language Engineering Research Group
of MIMOS Berhad, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.


a. Phonemes
-------------

The database uses SAMPA symbols to specify the phonemes.

Phoneme	 	SAMPA	Example

_		_	(silence)

[consonants]
p		p	pasang
b		b	besar
t		t	tidur
d		d	dekat
k		k	kasih
c		tS	cari
j		dZ	jarum
y		j	yang
g		g	ganti
h		h	hutan
f		f	faham
v,w		w	wira, versi
s		s	sopan
z		z	zalim
m		m	makan
n		n	nasi
r		r	roti
l		l	lekas

ng		N	ngeri
kh		H	khabar
sy		S	syabas
ny		nY	nyaman
gh		G	ghaib


[vowels]
a		V	ayah
e		@	emak
e (ay)		e	enak
e (air)		eI	ekspot
i		I	ini
o		@U	orang
o		Q	objek
u		U	untuk

ai		aI	balai
au		aU	pulau
oi		OI	amboi

Note : The eI and Q phonemes are not true Malay phonemes and
were only added to facilitate the pronounciation of borrowed
words. At the moment, there are some missing diphone pairs that
involve these 2 vowels in the database. If in doubt, use the e
and @U phonemes instead.
